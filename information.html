Foods

Tacos are a popular Spanish dish in Mexico. This dish is created with tortillas and other ingredients to put onto the tortilla. The fillings that you put inside the tortillas depend on you. You can put beef, pork, chicken, seafood, beans, vegetables, and cheese. It is optional to add in spice.

Enchiladas is a popular dish made with tortillas. The tortillas are rolled around the fillings and then poured with some sauce. The ingredients that you can put around the dough includes meat, cheese, beans, potatoes, and vegetables. Other ingredients can also be put in the tortillas.

Guacamole is a popular dish and can be considered a snack. It is often eaten with a tortilla chip. You need to dip the tortilla chip into the avocado. However, you will need to put the guacamole in an air-tight container or wrap it tightly. This will help prevent the sauce from becoming brown.

Chiles en nogada is a national Mexican dish. This dish uses poblano chiles stuffed with shredded meat or fruits. You can stuff it in with spices if you would like to. After stuffing whatever ingredient you decide to put it in, you can then put a walnut-creamed sauce on top of it.

Tamales are a traditional dish made from dough. The dough used to make tamales are from the outside of the corn. You can put many types of ingredients inside the dough. That includes meat, cheese, fruits, vegetables, herbs, and chillies. You can add other ingredients depending on your taste.

Churros is a dish made from dried dough. The size of the dough can be long and thin but it really depends on how you like it. Churros is usually eaten for breakfast. It is usually dipped in coffee or chocolate. Churros is usually a snack or dessert in the afternoon.




Culture & Tradition

Charro(Charra suit) is a type of suit that is popular in Mexico. The suit is based on a type of horseman named "The Charro". This suit can bring pride and nationalism to the country. Even though, it is a type of suit for a man, it can also be worn by a woman.

Huipiles is a popular dress that is worn by women. The dress is made from 2-3 rectangular pieces of fabric, ribbons, and fabric strips. The fabric is made out of cotton. These dresses are often worn during weddings. High and low rank women can wear these too.

The Day of The Dead is a traditional holiday celebrated by Mexicans. This holiday is celebrated on November 1st to November 2nd. It can be a multi-day holiday depending on the people. They can decide to celebrate the Day of The Dead on Otober 31st or November 6th.

Mexico Independence Day is a special holiday for Mexicans. It celebrates Mexico's victory against Spain. This holiday is celebrated on September 16th. This holiday is also simlar to America's Indepenence day and their way of celebrating it.




Famous Places

The Chichén Itzá is a city created from the Mayas. It is located in Tinúm Municipality, Yucatán State, Mexico. This site is one of the most visited sites in Mexico. The city contains many pyramids and ancient artifacts that tourist can come look at.

Cozumel is an island in the Carribean Sea. Since it is surrounded by water, it can be considered a peninsula depending on how you think of it. Marine life in the waters of Cozumel is quite a view to see. The beaches there look incredible and I would recommend taking a visit when out for vacation time.

Playa del Carmen is a city located along the Carribean Sea. This city is a very popular place for tourists to come. The population of the city is growing year by year. This place is well known for its beaches and marine life. The beaches there are incredibly beautiful.

The Riviera Maya is a very popular tourist site. It is a resort district that many people go to during the holidays or for vacation. The resorts have many fine dining restaurants that tourist usually go to. Not to exclude the fact that the huts there are also great.
